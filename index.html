Our Story

What is HEMA?

Historical European Martial Arts — learn to fight with swords, like medieval and renaissance people did.

H.E.M.A.

Historical
European
Martial Arts.

Short version: Learn how to fight with swords, like medieval and renaissance people did. Apply this skill with your friends and other enthusiasts in safe modern equipment. Get better physically and mentally.

Our club is open to everyone, even those who have never trained before. Our experienced instructors will help you regain your mobility, rejuvenate your fitness levels, and improve your coordination.

Through HEMA, we aim to inspire movement, build social connections, and have fun. We believe that the Sword is an excellent tool to help achieve these goals.

FAQ

What Are You
Most Interested In?

The Entry Course is a structured 2-month beginner program (16 sessions, 2× per week) designed for people with no prior HEMA experience. All equipment is provided. Regular Training is for those who want to continue training long-term — 3× per week, open format with sparring and free practice. Youth course is designed specifically for ages 15–17 with a curriculum tailored to younger students.
We offer a free trial lesson before you commit to any course. If after the first paid session you decide it's not for you, we handle refunds on a case-by-case basis. Please contact us at info@hema-praha.cz to discuss your situation.

HEMA is a contact sport, so some bruising is normal during sparring. However, we use modern safety equipment — padded jackets, fencing masks, and protective gloves — that significantly reduces injury risk. Our instructors are experienced and structure training progressively, so beginners start with technique drills before any contact work.
